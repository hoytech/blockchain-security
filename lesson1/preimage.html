Lab 1: Reversing a hash

I chose a secret number and this is its SHA1 hash: 
f0501dc19c8aa8925e88f6044d49555b575b65b5
What is my secret number?
HINT: I always choose numbers less than 1 million.
  1. Open the console (control-shift-j)
  2. In the console, run the following: 
    sha1("1")
  3. NOTE: The sha1 function needs strings, not numbers
